About Our Project

Revolutionizing agriculture through machine learning plant disease detection and treatment recommendations

Project Overview

The Crop Disease Detection System is an innovative web application that leverages machine learning to identify plant diseases from leaf images. Our system helps farmers, gardeners, and agricultural professionals quickly diagnose plant health issues and receive targeted treatment recommendations.

Using a machine learning model trained on a comprehensive dataset of 87,000+ plant images, our system can accurately detect 38 different classes of plant diseases and healthy conditions across various crop species.
